What is windshield repair?

Windshield repair fills chips and minor cracks with a clear resin that bonds to the glass. It is a quick, cost-effective option when the damage is small, away from critical viewing areas, and has not spread too far.

When this service helps

  • Repairing rock chips before Tucson heat and road vibration turn them into long cracks
  • Saving money compared with a full windshield replacement when repair is safe
  • Helping restore the strength of the damaged area when the glass is repairable
  • Handling common damage from gravel, road debris, and small impact points

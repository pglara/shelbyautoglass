What is adas calibration?

ADAS stands for Advanced Driver Assistance Systems. These systems can support features like Lane Departure Warning, Lane Keep Assist, Forward Collision Warning, Adaptive Cruise Control, and Automatic Emergency Braking. Shelby Auto Glass is Certified in Auto Glass ADAS Calibration.
